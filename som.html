SOM Architects (English: Skidmore, Owings & Merrill LLP, SOM) is an American architectural firm that provides design services to clients in architecture, interior design, urban design, structural engineering and civil engineering . The firm was founded in Chicago in 1936 by Louis Skidmore and Nathaniel Owings, joined by John Merrill in 1939, and opened its first office in New York, New York in 1937. glass box skyscrapers.2
